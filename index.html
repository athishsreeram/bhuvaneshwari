Temple History

Bhuvaneshwari (Sanskrit: भुवनेश्वरी, IAST: Bhuvaneśvarī) is the fourth amongst the ten Mahavidya goddesses and an aspect of Devi or Durga as elements of the physical cosmos in giving shape to the creation of the World. She is the Divine Mother as the Queen of all Worlds. The entire Universe is said to be her body and all beings are ornaments of her infinite being. She carries all the worlds as a flowering of her own Self-nature. She is thus related to Tripura Sundari, the supreme Lady of the Universe. She is also known as Adi Shakti or Parvati i.e. one of the earliest forms of shakti. She is capable of turning situations according to her wish. It is considered that even the navagrahas and Trimurti cannot stop her from doing anything.
